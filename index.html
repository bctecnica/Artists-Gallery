I was taught at Ealing School of Art where I was influenced by an evening class tutor in Life Drawing and Portraiture by the name of Frank Auerbach - now celebrated as an internationally renowned artist.

It was he who taught me how to see the reality of my subject rather than what I thought I was looking at.

Flights of fancy or abstraction have never been of interest to me. I like to paint and draw portraits, the nude figure, still life - including flowers - and if the weather is good, the occasional landscape.

I work in acrylics preferring it to oils for its quick drying properties.
